Data Sources

This dictionary documents the specific lexical and phonological features of the Burushaski dialect spoken in Kashmir. The vocabulary is curated from:

  • Hari Parbat Fieldwork: Primary data collected from the Burusho community residing in the Hari Parbat area of Srinagar.
  • Sadaf Munshi: Research and documentation by linguist Dr. Sadaf Munshi on the contact-induced changes in Srinagar Burushaski.
  • Community Interviews: Oral histories and daily speech recordings from elderly speakers in the Kashmiri diaspora.
  • Dialect Filtering: Rigorous exclusion of standard Hunza/Nagar forms to ensure only Kashmiri-specific variants and loanwords are presented.
